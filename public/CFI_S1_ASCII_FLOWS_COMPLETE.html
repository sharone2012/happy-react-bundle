                                                           NEVER recirculated to substrate

KEY GUARDRAILS (POS S1A):
═════════════════════════

1. ICP-OES Fe Gate (CFI-LAB-POME-001):
   Fe < 3,000 mg/kg DM      → S2 inclusion rate 20% WW
   Fe 3,000–5,000 mg/kg DM  → S2 inclusion rate 10–15% WW
   Fe 5,000–8,000 mg/kg DM  → S2 inclusion rate 5–10% WW
   Fe > 8,000 mg/kg DM      → CaCO₃ amendment required before S2

2. Capture Point (CRITICAL):
   POS = mill exit sludge (centrifuge discharge or sludge pit)
   POME ≠ POS (POME = liquid effluent to pond)
   Never conflate — lower Fe/Mn, higher N/P, residual CPO 5–20% DM

3. Press Water Routing:
   All centrifuge press water → POME pond ONLY
   Never recirculate to substrate (contamination risk)

4. Heavy Metals:
   Fe 3,000–8,000 mg/kg DM (typical range, no legal limit)
   Mn 100–475 mg/kg DM (typical range)
   SNI P0 blockers: Cd, Pb, As, Hg (require ICP-MS before registration)

KEY GUARDRAILS (EFB S1C):
═════════════════════════

1. Gate B.G2 (Press MC Floor):
   Post-press MC floor = 50% MAX (EFB line only)
   OPDC line: MC floor = 40% MIN (CLASS A hard clamp — never below)
   Math.max(40, reading) enforcement on OPDC

2. Gate B.G1 (Particle Size Target):
   Target: D90 ≤ 2mm (CLASS A locked)
   Vibrating screen ⑧ enforces via oversize reject loop to hammer mill ⑦
   Continuous recirculation until spec achieved

3. Hammer Mill Isolation (CRITICAL):
   Spring isolation ONLY — never rigid anchor to concrete slab
   Vibration protection mandatory (applies both EFB and OPDC lines)
   Failure mode: structural cracking, equipment damage

4. Press Water Routing:
   All press water → POME pond ONLY
   Never recirculate to substrate (contamination risk)

5. PKSA Dosing (inline during press):
   Rate: 6–10 kg/t EFB fresh weight
   Target pH: 10–12 (neutralisation to pH 6.5–8.0 occurs in S2 via 24hr dwell + CO₂ carbonation)
   60–90 min contact time in S2 mixing pugmill

6. Asian Equipment Derate:
   All nameplate capacities derated to 65% (hard rule across S1)
   Example: Hammer mill nameplate 15 t/h → derated 9.75 t/h working capacity

KEY GUARDRAILS (OPDC S1B):
══════════════════════════

1. Gate B.G2 (Press MC Floor — CRITICAL):
   OPDC press discharge MC floor = 40% MIN (CLASS A locked)
   Math.max(40, reading) enforcement — NEVER ALLOW MC < 40%
   Failure mode: pore damage → BSF colonisation fail → S4 process collapse
   
   **Why 40% is non-negotiable:**
   - OPDC cellular structure collapses below 40% MC
   - Pore network damage prevents BSF larvae penetration in S4
   - Larvae require 60–70% MC substrate with intact pore structure
   - Once collapsed, pores cannot re-open via re-wetting

2. Gate C.G2/G3 (pH Neutralisation — 24hr dwell):
   Target: pH 8.0–9.0 before S2 BSF transfer
   Process: 24hr dwell with 2 automated turnings (@ 8–12hr, @ 16–20hr)
   Mechanism: CO₂ carbonation (atmospheric uptake during turning)
   Input pH: 10–12 (from PKSA addition at press node ⑤)
   Output pH: 6.5–8.0 (final target range for BSF substrate)

3. Hammer Mill Isolation (CRITICAL):
   Spring isolation ONLY — never rigid anchor to concrete slab
   Vibration protection mandatory (applies both EFB and OPDC lines)
   Failure mode: structural cracking, equipment damage

4. Press Water Routing:
   All press water → POME pond ONLY
   Never recirculate to substrate (contamination risk)

5. PKSA Dosing (inline during press):
   Rate: 6–10 kg/t OPDC fresh weight
   Target pH: 10–12 (neutralisation to pH 6.5–8.0 occurs in buffer bin ⑩ via 24hr dwell)
   60–90 min contact time during S1 pressing + S2 mixing

6. Asian Equipment Derate:
   All nameplate capacities derated to 65% (hard rule across S1)
   Example: Hammer mill nameplate 12 t/h → derated 7.8 t/h working capacity

7. BSF Substrate Readiness:
   Post-24hr dwell: pH 8.0–9.0 · MC 60–62% · D90 ≤ 2mm · lignin reduced 30–35%
   BSF score: 4.275/5.0 (Conditional Pass) → expected Pass post-PKSA treatment
   Critical: pore structure intact (MC ≥ 40% enforcement prevents collapse)
