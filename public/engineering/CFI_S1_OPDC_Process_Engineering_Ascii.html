§14.4 OPDC S1B — Ascii Process Flow
① RCV-OPDC-01 — OPDC Receiving Hopper
IN: OPDC from mill 3-phase decanter discharge
Stream: 5 t/h · 70–75% MC · decanter cake
Storage: 20 m³ steel hopper · 60° walls · hydraulic gate
OUT: Gravity feed → CVB-OPDC-01 ②
② CVB-OPDC-01 — Drag Chain Conveyor
IN: OPDC from hopper ①
Stream: 5 t/h · 70–75% MC
Transport: 10m × 500mm belt · 5.5kW motor · VFD controlled · Elevation: +2.5m
OUT: Discharge → OSD-01 ③
③ OSD-01 — Primary Shredder
IN: OPDC cake from conveyor ②
Stream: 5 t/h · 70–75% MC · cake lumps
Process: Twin-shaft low-speed shredder · 2×22kW motors · 15–25 RPM
Action: Reduce to ~80–120mm fragments · No moisture change
OUT Stream: 5 t/h · 70–75% MC · fragments
OUT: → CVB-OPDC-02 ④
④ CVB-OPDC-02 — Incline Belt Conveyor
IN: Shredded fragments from OSD-01 ③
Stream: 5 t/h · 70–75% MC · fragments
Transport: 7m × 500mm cleated belt · 4kW · 30° incline · Elevation: +3.5m
OUT: Top discharge → BIN-OPDC-01 ⑤
⑤ BIN-OPDC-01 — Buffer Bin
IN: Fragments from incline ④
Stream: 5 t/h · 70–75% MC
Storage: 20 m³ steel bin · conical bottom · 4hr capacity
Discharge: VFD screw feeder to control downstream flow
OUT: Gravity + screw → OLB-01 ⑥
⑥ OLB-01 — Lump Breaker
IN: Fragments from buffer bin ⑤
Stream: 5 t/h · 70–75% MC · fragments
Process: Twin-roller lump breaker · 7.5kW · 25–40mm gap
Action: Break fragments to ~25–40mm pieces · No moisture change
OUT Stream: 5 t/h · 70–75% MC · smaller pieces
OUT: Chute → OHM-01 ⑦
⑦ OHM-01 — Hammer Mill
IN: Pieces from lump breaker ⑥ + oversize return from screen ⑧
Stream: 5 t/h primary + ~0.5 t/h recycle · 70–75% MC
Process: High-speed hammer mill · 22kW nameplate (14kW @ 65% Asian derate)
Screen: 2.0mm perforated · rotor 500mm Ø · 1200–1500 RPM
Isolation: Spring mounts only — never rigid anchor
Action: Reduce to D90 ≤2mm
OUT Stream: ~5 t/h · 70–75% MC · D90 ≤2mm
OUT: → OSC-01 ⑧
Oversize return: loops back to ⑦ — NEVER to ⑥ or ③
║ Parallel Unit: ODC-01 Baghouse — duct-fed dust collection from ⑦
║ Not inline — runs parallel to main flow
⑧ OSC-01 — Vibrating Screen
IN: Hammer mill discharge ⑦
Stream: ~5.5 t/h (incl. recycle) · 70–75% MC · mixed sizes
Process: Linear vibrating screen · 2×1.1kW motors · 2.0mm mesh
Split: Undersize (D90 ≤2mm) → OPR-01 ⑨
      Oversize (>2mm) → Return to OHM-01 ⑦ (~10% recycle fraction)
Undersize net: ~4.5 t/h · 70–75% MC · D90 ≤2mm
OUT: Undersize → OPR-01 ⑨
⑨ OPR-01 — Screw Press
IN: Screen undersize from OSC-01 ⑧
Stream: ~4.5 t/h · 70–75% MC · D90 ≤2mm
Process: Twin-screw dewatering press · 11kW
Action: Dewater 70–75% → 60–62% MC
Gate C.G1 Class A: Press floor MC ≥40% — Math.max(40, reading) enforced
Split: Cake → BIN-OPDC-301 ⑩ | Filtrate → POME pond
Cake Stream: ~3.21 t/h · 61% MC (mid of 60–62%)
OUT: Cake → BIN-OPDC-301 ⑩ | Filtrate → POME pond
⑩ BIN-OPDC-301 — S2 Buffer Bin
IN: Press cake from OPR-01 ⑨
Stream: ~3.21 t/h · 61% MC · D90 ≤2mm
Storage: 15 m³ concrete or carbon steel bin (6–10 t @ 45–65% MC)
Gate C.G1: MC ≥40% Class A floor enforced (Math.max hardcode)
Gate C.G2/G3: pH 8.0–9.0 + dwell ≥24hrs before S2 transfer
Status: S2 Feed Ready after pH neutralisation — amber marker
OUT: To Stage 2 biological inoculation
Mass Balance & Process Calculations — OPDC S1B
Core Formulas
Dry Matter
DM (t/h) = FW_in × (1 − MC_in / 100)
Cake Output at Target MC
FW_cake = DM / (1 − MC_cake / 100)
Water Removed → POME Pond
Water_removed = FW_press_feed − FW_cake
Mass Yield (press cake / press feed)
Yield (%) = (FW_cake / FW_press_feed) × 100
Gate C.G1 Floor
MC_out = Math.max(40%, measured_MC) ← hardcoded BSF pore protection
Screen Recycle (estimated 10%)
Net press feed ≈ 0.90 × FW hammer mill input

Gate C.G1 — Class A MC Floor + Gate C.G2/G3 pH/Dwell Compliance

OPDC has two sequential gate requirements before S2 transfer. Gate C.G1 is a hard floor; Gates C.G2/G3 are timed checks.

Gate Parameter Requirement Measured Value Enforced Value Status Action if Fail
C.G1 Class A MC Floor ≥ 40% 61% (design) Math.max(40, 61) = 61% PASS —
C.G1 Class A MC Floor ≥ 40% 35% (over-pressed) Math.max(40, 35) = 40% FLOORED Add moisture to 40% before S2
C.G2 pH 8.0 – 9.0 pH 8.5 (design) 8.5 PASS —
C.G2 pH 8.0 – 9.0 pH 7.2 7.2 FAIL Add lime · retest after 4hr
C.G3 Dwell time ≥ 24 hrs — — TIMED Hold in BIN-OPDC-301 until 24hr elapsed

C.G1 Class A floor protects BSF substrate pore structure from over-dewatering. C.G2/G3 ensure alkalinity and microbial conditioning.

Legend & Key Corrections
MC = Moisture Content (wet basis %)
FW = Fresh Weight (as-received wet mass)
DM = Dry Matter = FW × (1 − MC/100)
D90 = 90th percentile particle diameter ≤2mm target
VFD = Variable Frequency Drive
Asian derate = 65% of nameplate · OHM-01 runs 14kW not 22kW
Gate C.G1 Class A = Press floor MC ≥40% — Math.max(40, reading) hardcoded to prevent BSF pore damage from over-drying
Gate C.G2/G3 = pH 8.0–9.0 + dwell ≥24hrs + 2 turnings before S2 transfer
Oversize return = OSC-01 ⑧ rejects → OHM-01 ⑦ only — NEVER to OLB-01 ⑥ or OSD-01 ③
ODC-01 Baghouse = Parallel unit duct-fed from OHM-01 ⑦ — NOT inline
BIN-OPDC-301 material = Concrete or carbon steel (not stainless steel)
Filtrate routing = All press water → POME pond ONLY — never to substrate
Calc assumption = DM conserved through all mechanical steps · moisture change only at press · 10% screen recycle estimated
