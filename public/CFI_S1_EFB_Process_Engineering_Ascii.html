§14.3 EFB S1C — Ascii Process Flow
① RCV-EFB-01 — EFB Receiving Hopper
IN: Whole EFB bunches from mill unloading
Stream: 20 t/h · 62.5% MC · bunches + loose fibers
Storage: 50 m³ steel hopper · 60° walls · hydraulic gate
OUT: Gravity feed → CVB-EFB-01 ②
② CVB-EFB-01 — Drag Chain Conveyor
IN: EFB from hopper ①
Stream: 20 t/h · 62.5% MC
Transport: 12m × 600mm belt · 7.5kW motor · VFD controlled
Elevation: +2.5m lift to shredder feed
OUT: Discharge → ESD-01 ③
③ ESD-01 — Primary Shredder
IN: Whole bunches from conveyor ②
Stream: 20 t/h · 62.5% MC · bunches
Process: Twin-shaft low-speed shredder · 2×37kW motors · 15–25 RPM
Action: Reduce to ~100–150mm ribbons
OUT Stream: 20 t/h · 62.5% MC · ribbons
OUT: → CVB-EFB-02 ④
④ CVB-EFB-02 — Incline Belt Conveyor
IN: Shredded ribbons from ESD-01 ③
Stream: 20 t/h · 62.5% MC · ribbons
Transport: 8m × 600mm cleated belt · 5.5kW · 30° incline
Elevation: +4.0m lift to buffer bin
OUT: Top discharge → BIN-EFB-01 ⑤
⑤ BIN-EFB-01 — Buffer Bin
IN: Ribbons from incline ④
Stream: 20 t/h · 62.5% MC
Storage: 50 m³ steel bin · conical bottom · 2.5hr capacity
Discharge: VFD screw feeder to control downstream flow
OUT: Gravity + screw → ELB-01 ⑥
⑥ ELB-01 — Lump Breaker
IN: Ribbons from buffer bin ⑤
Stream: 20 t/h · 62.5% MC · ribbons
Process: Twin-roller lump breaker · 11kW · 30–50mm gap
Action: Break ribbons to ~30–50mm fragments
OUT Stream: 20 t/h · 62.5% MC · fragments
OUT: Chute → EHM-01 ⑦
⑦ EHM-01 — Hammer Mill
IN: Fragments from lump breaker ⑥ + oversize return from screen ⑧
Stream: 20 t/h primary + recycle · 62.5% MC
Process: High-speed hammer mill · 37kW nameplate (24kW @ 65% Asian derate)
Screen: 2.0mm perforated · rotor 600mm Ø · 1200–1500 RPM
Isolation: Spring mounts only — never rigid anchor
Action: Reduce to D90 ≤2mm
OUT Stream: 20 t/h · 62.5% MC · D90 ≤2mm powder
OUT: Screen undersize → ESC-01 ⑧
Oversize return: loops back to ⑦ hammer mill for re-milling
║ Parallel Unit: EDC-01 Baghouse — duct-fed dust collection from ⑦
║ Not inline — runs parallel to main flow
⑧ ESC-01 — Vibrating Screen
IN: Hammer mill discharge ⑦
Stream: 20 t/h · 62.5% MC · mixed particle sizes
Process: Linear vibrating screen · 2×1.5kW motors · 2.0mm mesh
Split: Undersize (D90 ≤2mm) → EPR-01 ⑨
      Oversize (>2mm) → Return to EHM-01 ⑦ for re-milling
Undersize Stream: ~18 t/h · 62.5% MC · D90 ≤2mm
OUT: Undersize → EPR-01 ⑨
⑨ EPR-01 — Screw Press
IN: Screen undersize from ESC-01 ⑧
Stream: ~18 t/h · 62.5% MC · D90 ≤2mm
Process: Twin-screw dewatering press · 15kW · 0.5mm screen gap
Action: Dewater 62.5% → 45–50% MC
Split: Cake (solids) → BIN-EFB-301 ⑩
      Filtrate (water) → POME pond
Gate B.G2: Press cake MC ≤50% before bin acceptance
Cake Stream: ~13 t/h · 45–50% MC · D90 ≤2mm
OUT: Cake → BIN-EFB-301 ⑩ | Filtrate → POME pond
⑩ BIN-EFB-301 — S2 Buffer Bin
IN: Press cake from EPR-01 ⑨
Stream: ~13 t/h · 45–50% MC · D90 ≤2mm
Storage: 50 m³ steel bin · 2.5hr buffer at S2 rate
Gate B.G2: MC ≤50% enforced
Status: S2 Feed Ready — teal marker
OUT: To Stage 2 biological inoculation
Legend & Key Corrections
MC = Moisture Content (wet basis %)
D90 = 90th percentile particle diameter ≤2mm target
VFD = Variable Frequency Drive
Asian derate = 65% of nameplate capacity (EHM-01 runs 24kW not 37kW)
Gate B.G2 = Press cake MC ≤50% before BIN-EFB-301 acceptance
Oversize return = ESC-01 ⑧ screen rejects loop back to EHM-01 ⑦ for re-milling — NEVER to ELB-01 ⑥ or ESD-01 ③
EDC-01 Baghouse = Parallel unit duct-fed from EHM-01 ⑦ — NOT inline in main flow
Filtrate routing = All press water + wash water → POME pond ONLY — never to substrate
EHM-01 nameplate = 65% of nameplate · 37kW motor (not "65% of 23kW")
