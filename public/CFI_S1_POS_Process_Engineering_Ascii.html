§14.2 POS S1A — Ascii Process Flow
① PIT-POS-01 — Pos Reception Pit
IN: POS sludge from mill decanter discharge via truck
Stream: ~30 t/day fresh · 80–92% MC · decanted slurry
Storage: 15 m³ at-grade RC concrete hopper
Dimensions: L:3.5m W:2.5m H:2.0m · 60° sloped walls
Material: RC concrete body + epoxy coating (oil-resistant)
Lip: EL +0.8–1.0m above grade for truck discharge
Drainage: 150mm bottom valve → POME pond · deep sump
OUT: Pump suction → T-SLD-101 ②
② T-SLD-101 — Sludge Holding Tank
IN: POS from pit ① via pump
Stream: ~1.25 t/h average · 80–92% MC
Storage: 5–8 m³ vertical SS304 tank · sealed dome top
Agitator: 3.7 kW slow-speed paddle · VFD controlled
Dwell: ~6–12hr homogenization before decanter
Vent: Biofilter odor control (optional if complaints)
Elevation: EL 0.0m ground level
OUT: Controlled feed → PMP-POS-01 ③
③ PMP-POS-01 — Sludge Transfer Pump
IN: Homogenized sludge from T-SLD-101 ②
Stream: 1.5–2.5 m³/h variable · 80–92% MC
Process: Progressive cavity pump · SS316L wetted parts
Motor: 5.5 kW · 3-phase · VFD for flow control
Head: 15m TDH (lifts to +2.5m platform)
OUT: Discharge → DCN-POS-01 ④ at elevation +2.5m
④ DCN-POS-01 — Decanter Centrifuge
IN: Sludge from pump ③
Stream: 1.5 t/h · 82% MC (post-homogenization)
Process: 3-phase decanter centrifuge · 11kW main motor + auxiliaries
Platform: EL +2.5m vibration-isolated steel structure
Manufacturer: Alfa Laval preferred for 3-phase performance
Separation: Cake + water + residual oil phases
Split: Cake (65% MC) → BUF-POS-01 ⑤
      Water + oil → POME pond
Cake Stream: ~0.55 t/h · 65% MC
OUT: Cake → BUF-POS-01 ⑤ | Liquids → POME pond
⑤ BUF-POS-01 — S2 Buffer Bin
IN: Decanter cake from DCN-POS-01 ④
Stream: ~0.55 t/h · 65% MC · ~13.5 t/day
Storage: 5 m³ steel bin · 8–12hr buffer
Material: Carbon steel + epoxy · navy S2 marker
Gate: ICP-OES Fe result per CFI-LAB-POME-001 protocol
   Fe <3,000 mg/kg DM → 20% WW blend rate
   Fe 3,000–5,000 → 10–15% blend
   Fe 5,000–8,000 → 5–10% + CaCO₃ amendment
   Fe >8,000 → reject batch
Status: S2 Feed Ready after Fe gate clearance
OUT: To Stage 2 biological inoculation (blend ratio per Fe result)
Legend & Key Specifications
MC = Moisture Content (wet basis %)
POS = Palm Oil Sludge (decanter discharge slurry) — NOT POME liquid effluent
TDH = Total Dynamic Head
ICP-OES Fe gate = Protocol CFI-LAB-POME-001 determines POS inclusion rate based on Fe concentration
PIT-POS-01 = At-grade hopper (not in-ground) · epoxy-coated RC concrete (not SS316L) · 60° sloped walls · 150mm bottom drain valve
T-SLD-101 = 5–8 m³ · SS304 · 3.7kW agitator · sealed dome · biofilter vent optional
