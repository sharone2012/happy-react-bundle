## 🎯 KEY INSIGHTS BY SOIL:

### **INCEPTISOL (39% IDN palms — MOST COMMON)**

**Top performers:**
1. **Phanerochaete sp. (4.8★)** — lignin reduction 35-45% (vs 25% Oxisol); optimal on low P-fixation
2. **Aspergillus niger (4.5★)** — citric acid + cellulase dual benefit; pH 5.0 optimal
3. **Trichoderma harzianum (4.5★)** — cellulase 85-95% activity pH 5.0; biocontrol Ganoderma

**Strategy:** Maximize high-performance organisms on already-healthy soil. Well-aerated conditions favor white-rot fungi + aerobic N-fixers.

---

### **OXISOL (8% IDN palms — EXTREME P-fixation 65%)**

**Top performers:**
1. **Aspergillus niger (4.8★)** — citric acid (pKa 3.1) chelates gibbsite/goethite P; 45-67% release
2. **Acaulospora scrobiculata (4.5★)** — acid-specialist AMF; P-uptake bypass gibbsite fixation
3. **Bacillus megaterium (4.2★)** — gluconic acid (pKa 3.6) Fe/Al oxide P-release

**Strategy:** Extreme P-solubilisers CRITICAL. Liming mandatory (1.5 t/ha → pH 4.4→5.0). Nitrosomonas post-lime only.

**⚠️ CRITICAL:** Nitrosomonas sp. (3.5★) — nitrification 5→23 mg N/kg/d post-lime (4.6× increase); NEVER apply pre-lime.

---

### **HISTOSOL / PEAT (7% IDN palms — waterlogged, N2O risk)**

**Top performers:**
1. **Lactobacillus sp. (4.2★ SAFE)** — N-trap 7-15 kg N/t DM; CH4 suppression 70-80%; pH 3.8 compatible; NO N2O risk
2. **Cellulomonas fimi (4.0★)** — facultative anaerobe; cellulase O2 <10%; peat degradation viable
3. **Bacillus subtilis (3.8★)** — aerobic zone pioneer; CFI PRIMARY VALUE on peat

**Strategy:** CFI creates aerobic zones in peat (S0-S3). Aerobic zone establishment = key value proposition.

**🚨 N2O RISK — LOCKED:**
- **Azotobacter vinelandii (2.0★)** — N2O amplification 5→15+ kg/ha/yr; LOCKED for safety
- **Azospirillum brasilense (2.0★)** — denitrification pathway active waterlogged peat; LOCKED

**✅ SAFE alternatives:**
- Lactobacillus + Saccharomyces (anaerobic-compatible, NO N2O risk, CH4 benefit)

---

### **SPODOSOL (rare — sandy 85%, CEC 6 cmol/kg LOWEST)**

**Top performers:**
1. **Lactobacillus sp. (4.5★)** — N-leaching combat (32% HIGHEST); N-trap 7-15 kg N/t DM
2. **Bacillus mucilaginosus (4.5★)** — EPS → CEC 6→9+ cmol/kg; silicate weathering K
3. **Azotobacter vinelandii (4.0★)** — alginate EPS → WHC 58→75mm; slow-release N

**Strategy:** EPS producers CRITICAL for CEC/WHC improvement on quartz sand. N-retention organisms combat 32% leaching.

---

### **ANDISOL (rare — volcanic, allophane 30%, P-fixation 82% HIGHEST)**

**Top performers:**
1. **Glomus mosseae (5.0★ ONLY perfect score!)** — AMF hyphae BYPASS allophane fixation; 25%→60% colonization
2. **Glomus intraradices (4.8★)** — P-uptake enhancement; AMF bypass allophane; 25%→55%
3. **Aspergillus niger (4.7★)** — citric acid + Si amendment synergy → P-fixation 82%→45%

**Strategy:** AMF bypass mechanism + Si amendment (CaSiO3 1.0 t/ha) synergy MANDATORY. Highest P-fixation of all 6 soils.

**✅ Si synergy organisms:** Aspergillus, Bacillus megaterium, Pseudomonas fluorescens
