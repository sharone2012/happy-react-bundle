WHAT MAKES PALM RESIDUES VALUABLE FOR SOIL?

CFI processes 12 palm oil residues into premium organic biofertilisers. Every residue delivers a distinct soil benefit profile — from EFB's unmatched potassium and organic matter, to OPDC's balanced NPK, to PKE's best-in-class phosphorus and trace elements.

Macronutrients (NPK): OPDC and PKE lead on nitrogen (2.3–3.2% N DM) — comparable to high-quality poultry manure. EFB leads on potassium (2.0–2.4% K DM = 24–29 kg K₂O/t DM). PKE leads on phosphorus (0.24–0.79% P DM), critical for P-fixing Oxisol and Andisol soils across Sumatra and Kalimantan.

Micronutrients: OPF leads for Mn (100–300 mg/kg) and B (15–25 mg/kg) — both commonly deficient in intensively managed oil palm. PKE leads for Zn (50–100 mg/kg) and Cu (20–35 mg/kg). OPDC has the highest Fe (2,000–10,000 mg/kg). The full CFI blended product delivers the complete micronutrient spectrum in a single application.

Critical blending principle: EFB alone fails SNI 19-7030-2004 — its C:N of 49–70 is too high for direct application. Blending EFB + OPDC at 70:30 brings C:N to 25–30 and boosts N+P to compliant levels. Adding PKE enriches P and trace elements. BSF frass adds chitin. The result is a complete certified organic biofertiliser superior to any single residue alone.
